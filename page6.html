The three witches meet with Hecate, who is the goddess of witchcraft. Hecate is angered at how the witches messed with Macbeth without asking her, and she tells the witches that she is now in charge of Macbeth's issues.
As they are speaking, hired murders are being sent to kill Banquo and his Son. Inside, he is fighting with his conscience, and regretting his actions but also doubling down and continuing. He has become a broken man, always paranoid and fearful. He now feels he needs to protect Lady Macbeth by not telling her what he did.
Lady Macbeth is feeling more doubts about herself and Macbeth. Before, she was the wicked and evil one, now she is the one with reservations.
